1. Service Description

BahiFlow is an automated B2B collections engine that sends payment reminders to your customers via WhatsApp on your behalf. The service includes:

  • Automated multi-tier payment reminders (6 escalation tiers over 35+ days)

  • Payment links via Razorpay and UPI deep links
  • Daily summary reports and morning action reports

  • Invoice dispute handling and customer commitment tracking
